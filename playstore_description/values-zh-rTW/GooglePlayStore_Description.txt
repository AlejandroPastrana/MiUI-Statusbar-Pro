####---#  Play Store full description
With MiUI Statusbar Pro you can change the style of your MiUI Statusbar. Our style modifications do not require xposed framework or conflict with MiUI Themes.
It all started with the well known "Center Clock Mod" and now you can chose between 7 different Designs!

Features:
**Creating Backups of MiuiSystemUI.apk
**Restoring Backups of MiuiSystemUI.apk
**Change the style of your MiUI Statusbar to 7 different Designs
**Also working with non-Xiaomi devices running MiUI!
**No xposed required !
 
Requirements:
**App needs Root-Access
**Only available for MiUI Firmwares
